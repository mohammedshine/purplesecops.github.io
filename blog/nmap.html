Nmap Cheatsheet

  • Normal Usage: nmap target.com
  • Version Discovery: nmap -sV target.com
  • UDP Scan: nmap -sU target.com
  • OS Detection: nmap -O target.com
  • Verbose: nmap -v target.com
  • Output in all 3 formats: nmap -oA target.com
  • Normal Output: nmap -oN target.com
  • Output in greppable format: nmap -oG target.com
  • Agressive Mode: nmap -A target.com
  • Timing Template: nmap -T5 target.com
